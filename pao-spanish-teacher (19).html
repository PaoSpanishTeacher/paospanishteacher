✨ Online Spanish Teacher · ELE

Learn Spanish with Pao: understand, don't memorize.

Hi, I'm Pao — Spanish as a Foreign Language teacher with a communicative approach based on storytelling and neuroeducation. I teach online to adults at all levels, from A1 to C2. Classes that make you speak from day one. ♥

My method · Mi método
Método Español Significativo

Comprehensible input · Storytelling · Neuroeducation · Meaningful learning. Language is acquired through emotion, context and real communication — never memorization. ♥
